📖 Metrics Glossary — what every column / number means (click to expand)
📊 Headline metrics
  • Composite — KEEP/DISCARD decision metric. Formula: min(test_sharpe, val_sharpe) − 0.05 × n_negative_folds. Punishes models that look great on test but fail on val (or vice-versa) AND models with regime-specific failures. Higher = better.
  • Test Sharpe — annualised Sharpe ratio on the held-out test fold (union of 7 regime windows: GFC, EU debt, taper, China-oil, vol-mageddon, COVID, AI rally). √252 × mean(daily_returns) / std(daily_returns). Sharpe > 1 = strong; > 2 = exceptional; QQQ buy-and-hold ≈ 1.22.
  • Val Sharpe — Sharpe on validation set (used for early-stopping + composite). Strong divergence from test Sharpe = overfitting.
  • Train Sharpe — Sharpe on training data. Diagnostic only — train sharpe much greater than test sharpe = severe overfit.
  • $Equity — final portfolio value if you started with $1,000 and traded the strategy across all 7 test folds. $3,144 in exp 52 = $1k → $3.1k = +214%.
🎯 Direction-prediction (classification)
  • Hit% — directional accuracy: percent of trade-days where sign(prediction) = sign(actual_return). 50% = coin-flip; 52-55% in finance is genuinely tradeable.
  • Prec / Rec — Precision / Recall on the UP-direction class. Precision = "of all UP predictions, how many were right." Recall = "of all actual UP days, how many did we catch."
  • F1 / F2 — F-scores. F1 = harmonic mean of precision and recall. F2 weights recall 2× more than precision (penalises missed UP-days harder; useful in trending markets).
  • MCC — Matthews Correlation Coefficient, a balanced accuracy metric robust to class imbalance. Range −1 to +1; 0 = random; > 0.1 = real signal in finance.
  • Acc — overall accuracy = (TP + TN) / total. Same as hit% but explicit.
📐 Statistical reliability
  • PSR — Probabilistic Sharpe Ratio (Bailey & López de Prado 2012). Probability that the OBSERVED Sharpe is greater than 0 given the sample size, skewness, and kurtosis. PSR > 0.95 = "very confident the Sharpe is positive, not noise." PSR = 1.000 means we are statistically certain. Critical because in-sample Sharpes can be inflated by skew/kurtosis or short windows.
  • IC — Information Coefficient = Pearson correlation between predicted return and actual return. Range −1 to +1. > 0.05 = real signal in equity prediction; > 0.10 = strong (Grinold-Kahn rule of thumb).
  • excess_sharpe — Strategy Sharpe MINUS buy-and-hold Sharpe over the same window. >0 = strategy beats holding the index. exp 52 +0.10 means +0.10 Sharpe over QQQ buy-and-hold (1.22 → 1.32).
🤖 Uncertainty heads (Kendall-Gal 2017)
  • Conf — Confidence = 1 − epistemic_uncertainty. The model's "I know the answer" signal. High conf + correct = good; high conf + wrong = dangerous overconfidence.
  • Ale — Aleatoric uncertainty (data noise estimate). Predicted log-variance from the het-loss head, exp-transformed. High aleatoric on a regime = "this period is genuinely noisy / unpredictable" (e.g. crisis windows). Optimal range 0.05-0.15.
  • epistemic — Model's "what I don't know yet" signal (typically MC-Dropout variance). High epistemic on a regime = "I haven't seen enough data of this type" — useful for regime-shift detection.
🧪 Experiment tracking
  • T+/V+ — count of positive folds in TEST / VALIDATION. 7/7 on test = perfect regime coverage; 3/7 = model fails on 4 of 7 regimes. Critical for ensemble and risk control.
  • Status — KEEP (composite improved) / DISCARD (composite regressed) / CRASH (training failed).
  • Time — wall-clock training time. ~30 min typical for Mamba 100-epoch run on RTX 4090 Laptop.
🎯 Targets (A / B / D)
  • A — fwd_ret_1d (PRIMARY) — 1-day forward log-return. Drives KEEP/DISCARD. The trade is realised on this return.
  • B — fwd_ret_5d — 5-day forward return (auxiliary head, jointly trained). Tracked because longer horizons are sometimes more stable.
  • D — vol-adjusted 1d — fwd_ret_1d / rolling_vol_20. Orthogonalises trend from skill — high D-Sharpe = real edge, not just trend-following. The TRADE is always on raw 1d return; D's prediction only sets DIRECTION.
📁 7-Fold walk-forward regime windows
  • F1 GFC peak crash (Lehman + Mar-2009 bottom)
  • F2 2011 US-downgrade + EU debt crisis
  • F3 Taper tantrum and 2014 H1
  • F4 China devaluation and oil crash 2015-2016
  • F5 2018 Vol-mageddon + Q4 sell-off
  • F6 COVID crash and V-recovery 2020
  • F7 Inflation bear, AI rally and 2025
Each fold tests a structurally different regime. A model with all 7 folds positive = robust across crisis types; few-fold positive = regime-specialised.
🎛️ Decision rules (CLAUDE.md research-strict)
  • KEEP — composite > previous global champion AND all 7 test folds positive.
  • NEAR-MISS — composite within 0.12 of champion (between 1.20 and 1.32 currently). Worth multi-seed lock.
  • DISCARD — otherwise. Composite + per-fold both regressed.
  • Multi-seed lock — single-seed lifts require ≥3 seeds with median > baseline before declaring champion. dmamba seed=42 has a "lucky basin" — both seq=90 (+0.96) and bs=16 (+1.51) at single-seed COLLAPSED to 3-seed median +0.14 / -0.24. Multi-seed median is the credible metric.
